Camera and Lens Recommendations

Beginner Recommend

DSLR: Canon - T3i to T7i (Depending on your budget) with 18-55mm lens or 55-200mm for more zoom Nikon - D3500 Point and Shoot: Sony DSC-RX100 Versions III-VI (depending on budget) Programs: Adobe Lightroom Book Recommendation: Stunning Digital Photography - Tony Northrup

Advance Recommend

DSLR: Canon 6d Mark II to Canon 5d Mark IV (depending on price. Older models are also good (Canon 6D, Canon 5D Mark III) Sony: Alpha A7 II Programs: Adobe Lightroom and Adobe Photoshop Other Equipment: Invest in good lighting, use Understanding Exposure - Bryan Peterson for help. Lenses - Use “Tony Northrup's Photography Buying Guide: How to Choose a Camera, Lens, Tripod, Flash, and More” for help depending on what specific kind of photography you would like to specialize in.
